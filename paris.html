First Two Nights

Paris Las Vegas

A mini-Paris chapter on the Strip with tower lights, café energy, French-style details, and easy first-night photo missions.

Cool Things to Know
🗼

46 Stories Up

The Eiffel Tower Viewing Deck rises 46 stories above the Strip.

📏

Half-Scale Tower

The tower is a half-scale replica, which makes it easy to spot from far away.

✨

Night Lights

The light show runs every 30 minutes from sunset to midnight.

Mini Paris Route

A simple hotel walk mission that works even if everyone is tired from travel.

1. Tower Photo

Find the best Eiffel Tower angle and take the first “we made it” picture.

2. Café Details

Spot signs, awnings, restaurant fronts, or French-style details.

3. Fountain View

Look toward the Bellagio side and see if you can catch the fountains.

4. Night Lights

Watch the tower sparkle after dark if everyone still has energy.
