Flight Mission Timeline

Step by step

Boarding

Find the seats, buckle up, headphones nearby, and decide what to watch first.

Pushback

The plane may move backward and stop. That is normal. The crew is getting lined up.

Takeoff Roll

The plane gets loud and fast. That is engine power doing its job.

Climb

Your stomach may feel weird for a few seconds. Keep breathing. This part passes.

Cruise

This is the easy zone: movie, game, snack, water, and window check.

Landing

Flaps, gear sounds, bumps, and braking are normal. Landing Legend badge unlocked.

What Is Normal?

Plane Sounds Decoder

  • Engine roar: power for takeoff.
  • Dings: crew messages.
  • Clunks: wheels, flaps, or normal plane parts moving.
  • Bumps: air-road bumps, like a car on a rough road.
