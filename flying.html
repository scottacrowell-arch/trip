For Lincoln

Flight Confidence Plan

Saturday 11:00 AM flight home. Nervous is allowed. We just need a plan.

Takeoff

The plane goes fast, tilts up, and your stomach may feel weird for a few seconds. That is normal.

Normal sounds

Engines, wheels going up, flaps moving, dings, bumps, and air blowing are all normal plane sounds.

Turbulence

Bumps in the air are like bumps in the road. Seat belt on, breathe, and keep doing your activity.

Dad check

Ask: “Is this normal?” Dad will tell you the truth and help you reset.

Lincoln’s Reset Plan
  1. 1
    Say it

    “I’m nervous. Help me reset.”

  2. 2
    Feet flat

    Put both feet on the floor and back against the seat.

  3. 3
    Box breathe

    Breathe in 4. Hold 4. Out 4. Hold 4.

  4. 4
    Pick a focus

    Movie, game, music, snack, or looking out the window.

  5. 5
    Ask again

    “Is this still normal?” Then keep going.
