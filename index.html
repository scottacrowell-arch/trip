Pick up the rental car at 9:00 AM, stop at Hoover Dam, check in to the condo at 4:00 PM, take a pool reset, then aim for a Zion picnic dinner.

Pack Smart

What to have ready.

Zion / Narrows

  • Narrows shoes and neoprene socks
  • Walking sticks or poles
  • Dry bag or zip bags
  • Extra socks and dry clothes
  • Water and electrolytes
  • Sunscreen and hats

Vegas / Travel

  • Swimsuits
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Phone chargers / battery pack
  • Light layer for arena and airport AC
  • Friday night airport clothes ready
  • Headphones for Lincoln’s flight plan
