Narrows Readiness

  • Narrows shoes ready
  • Walking sticks or poles ready
  • Water bottles filled
  • Dry clothes packed
  • Snacks packed
  • River and weather conditions checked

Safety Rule

Check river and weather before The Narrows. Do not force The Narrows if conditions, timing, or energy are off.
