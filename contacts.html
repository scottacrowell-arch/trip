Important Safety Info

If We Get Separated

This page is for the boys. Stop walking. Stay where you are. Find a safe adult who works there — hotel staff, park ranger, airport worker, security guard, police officer, or store employee — and show them this page.

What To Do First

1. Stop moving

Do not keep wandering. Stay in one safe, visible place.

2. Look around

Check for Dad, Mom, your brother, a hotel desk, ranger, security, or employee.

3. Ask a safe adult

Say: “I’m lost. Can you call my parents?”

4. Show this page

Use the contact card below so they can call or email.
