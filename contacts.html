If Lost

Stop. Stay. Ask.

Stop walking. Stay where you are. Find a safe adult who works there and show them this page.

Say This

“I’m lost. Can you help me call my dad or mom?”

Safe adults include hotel staff, park rangers, airport workers, security guards, police officers, or store employees.
